# AWS Foundations - Day 1

## What is a Region?
A region is a geographic area containing multiple isolated data centers.

## What is an Availability Zone (AZ)?
An AZ is an isolated data center within a region

## Why separate AZs?
To provide fault tolerance and high availability.

## What is IAM?
Identity and Access Management. It controls who can access AWS resources and what actions they can perform.

## Shared Responsibility Model
AWS secures the cloud infrastructure.
I secure what I put inside the cloud.
